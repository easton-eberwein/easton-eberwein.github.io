Who I Am

I’m a former Division I athlete turned analyst and problem solver. Competing at a high level taught me the value of discipline, resilience, and teamwork—qualities I now bring into my work in technology and analytics. With a background in business analytics and information systems, I enjoy breaking down complex challenges, uncovering insights in data, and turning them into solutions that make a real impact.

Outside of work, I still thrive on competition and growth—whether that’s running, lifting weights, exploring new skills, or building side projects. My goal is to bring the same focus I had on the track into creating digital tools and strategies that help people and organizations perform at their best.

My approach combines technical precision with creative problem-solving to deliver solutions that not only work flawlessly but also engage and delight users.
